God's Own Country
             Kerala is always referred to as God's own country. This term has a mythological belief
                    that states that- Lord Parshuram, a manifestation of Lord Vishnu threw his axe
                    into thevast sea to develop land for his followers to reside peacefully.
                    And that is how the state of Kerala was born from the god itself.
